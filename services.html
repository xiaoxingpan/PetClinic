Services we Provide



Veterinary Exams

Routine veterinary exams are an important part of any pet parents' schedule. This helps detect and prevent any problems you may face. Our team's main concern is in enhancing the lives of your pet friends.


Spay and Neutering

These routine surgeries help eliminate your pet's risk of developing uterine, ovarian and testicular cancer, as well as other forms of cancers and infections. It will also lessen aggressive or problematic behaviors, including separation anxiety, roaming, guarding and spraying.


Vaccinations

Getting your pet vaccinated is one of the most important ways to show you care. These injections are painless, and will protect them from a wide variety of serious conditions. Keeping track of your pet's vaccines is where we come in.


Bloodwork

Taking samples of your pet's blood is a routine part of their healthcare. This helps our team establish their baseline levels, diagnose issues, and monitor ongoing illnesses.


Nutrition Counselling

There are a lot of misconceptions in the pet owner world when it comes to your pet's eating and nutrition habits. Our team is dedicated to helping clear up any myths regarding this aspect of your pet's health.


Senior Care

However old your pet behaves, it is important to pivot their medical care for their elderly needs at this point in their life. We are here to make sure your pet stays as healthy as possible during this time. Don't hesitate to reach out for more information.
