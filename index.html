About us

You and your pet should feel comfortable. We give you and your pet(s) a tour around the clinic, This would be a great time to ask as many questions as you want about the services, fees, accommodations, and specialists available. You will also see how your pet reacts to the veterinarian, staff, and other animals. The staff and veterinarian are knowledgeable about pet care so you can be confident of your pets' safety We offer the extra services that fit with your lifestyle, for example If you travel frequently, you may want to consider boarding arrangements through your clinic, recommended by a trusted veterinarian. Emergencies happen so it is best to be prepared ahead of time. We are open 24/7; there are some vets who make house calls even in the middle of the night. We keep records of your pets' vaccinations and we remind you once your pet is due for more. We are here when you need us.
